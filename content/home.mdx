# RISE8 Companies

A vertically integrated real estate investment and operations firm headquartered in Boca Raton, Florida — and owner of the **Stayable** extended-stay hotel brand.

## Our companies

- **Stayable** — extended-stay hospitality across Florida, built on low rates and attentive, family-like service.
- **RISE8 Management** — hotel and apartment property management; operates the Stayable portfolio.
- **RISE8 Real Estate Finance** — senior secured loans, mezzanine, and preferred equity in the lodging sector.
- **RISE8 Development** — licensed general contractor for affiliated projects, from planning through delivery.

## Fall seven times, rise eight

Our name comes from the Japanese proverb *Nana korobi, ya oki* — a commitment to persistence that runs through how we invest, build, and operate.
